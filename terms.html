General Rules & Policies

Terms & Conditions

1. Scope of Application

These General Terms and Conditions (GTC) apply to all services offered by Maison Vélure GmbH at our premises located at Rungedamm 38, Hamburg, as well as any online consultation or appointment booking services.

2. Appointments and Cancellations

Appointments are binding once confirmed. We reserve a specific time slot exclusively for you. Therefore, we ask that you adhere to the following cancellation policy:

  • Cancellations made 24 hours or more in advance are entirely free of charge.
  • For cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance, we reserve the right to charge 50% of the scheduled service cost.
  • In case of a "no-show" without prior notice, 100% of the service cost may be charged.

3. Pricing and Payment

All prices listed on our website and in-salon menus include the statutory value-added tax (VAT). Prices are subject to change depending on the actual length, thickness of the hair, and specific materials used during coloring or treatments.

Payment is due immediately after the service is rendered. We accept Cash, EC Cards, Visa, and Mastercard.

4. Liability

Maison Vélure GmbH accepts no liability for items of clothing or personal property lost or damaged within the premises, unless the damage is the result of gross negligence or intentional harm by our staff.

5. Guarantee and Complaints

We strive to deliver the highest standard of service. If you are dissatisfied with a service, please inform us within 3 days of the appointment. We will offer a complimentary correction if the complaint is deemed justified. No refunds are provided for services rendered.
